About Me

 

My name is David Beauchamp. I'm a front-end developer in Oakland, CA who focuses on writing clean, elegant, and efficient code.

I have 5 years commercial experience working with clients from around the world, providing responsive front-end development and WordPress integrations. The work I provide is of highest quality, fully responsive, and tested in a wide range of devices. I take great care to ensure each project is well-documented and easily maintainable.

What I do

Responsive

Provide an optimal viewing experience—easy reading and navigation with a minimum of resizing, panning, and scrolling—across a wide range of devices.

Modern

Using the latest markup and style techniques for proper semantics and usability. Everything is optimized for browser compatibility and SEO.

Cross-browser

Mostly just making sure that everything works in Internet Explorer.

CMS

In many projects, client’s require the ability to manage the content of their website. Integrating front-end templates into custom WordPress themes with custom functionality via the WordPress CMS.
